#projet 1
titre_projet_1 | Inverse and robust design of joints using modeling tools of bio-inspired joints \
descriptif_projet_1_partie_1 | This project aims at reverse design engineering nature to obtain bio-inspired mechanical joints. These joints must be made up of contact surfaces kinematically compatible with the movements of the joint. \
descriptif_projet_1_partie_2 | Biological mechanical joints are both hyperstatic, to better distribute the transmitted forces, and made of complex surface elements. Today, numerical methods such as finite elements allow to model those contacts undependently of the degree of hyperstaticity of the joint. This allows to create a new mode of design of hyperstatic systems. \

#projet 2
titre_projet_2 | Fabrication, control and validation of bio-inspired joints \
descriptif_projet_2_partie_1 | This theme is about the production and control of bio-inspired mechanical joints based on complex surfaces of high geometric quality and surface finish. Tests will be carried out to validate the operation and the service life of the created links. \
descriptif_projet_2_partie_2 | The scientific challenges of this theme lie in the transition from the theoretical model to reality. The manufacturing and tests require the development of new strategies of finishing of the complex surfaces as well as the development of experiments to measure the performances of the complex joints (pressure of contact, wear, ...). \
